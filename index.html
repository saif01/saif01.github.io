cpbit_pg

Platform Gateway (PG) - the single pane of glass for CP Bangladesh.

The README outlines a platform that consolidates access management, attendance automation, QR initiatives, mobile app services, documentation hubs and notification pipelines. I lead its roadmap and delivery.

  • Unified authentication, Sanctum sessions and LDAP RBAC
  • Operations dashboards with KPI cards and widget builder
  • Biometric attendance ingestion, COM-port processing and exports
  • Mobile app APIs with OTA version control and content management
  • QR and badge lifecycle management with reporting
  • Notification services: GP CMP SMS, Telegram, email templates

Architecture from README

Backend: Laravel 12, PHP 8.2, Artisan commands for attendance, log consolidation, Gemini/OpenAI services and COM-port integrations.

Frontend: Vue 3 SPA with Vuetify 3, Vue Router, Vuex, Vite and Ziggy driven routing.

Reporting: maatwebsite/excel, barryvdh/laravel-snappy, Spatie PDF tools.

Integrations: LDAPRecord, Google Gemini, OpenAI, Telegram Bot SDK, GP CMP SMS, Oracle via Yajra OCI8.
