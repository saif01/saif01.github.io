Flagship

cpbit_pg - Platform Gateway

Summarized directly from cpbit_pg/README.md.

What it does

Central operations hub that unifies authentication, attendance automation, QR initiatives, mobile app services, document catalogues and notification pipelines.

  • LDAP/Active Directory + Sanctum powered RBAC
  • Operations dashboards with widget builder and KPI cards
  • Biometric attendance ingestion, COM-port readers, Gemini/OpenAI assistants
  • Mobile release APIs with OTA control and content management
  • QR/badge lifecycle management, inventory, document catalogues
  • Notification stack with GP CMP SMS, Telegram and email templates

Architecture & Stack

Backend: Laravel 12, PHP 8.2, Artisan commands for attendance sync, log consolidation, COM-port jobs and AI integrations.

Frontend: Vue 3 + Vuetify SPA, Vue Router, Vuex, Vite, Ziggy and Axios.

Reporting: maatwebsite/excel, barryvdh/laravel-snappy, Spatie PDF tools.

Integrations: LDAPRecord, Google Gemini, OpenAI, Telegram Bot SDK, GP CMP SMS, Oracle via Yajra OCI8.

Laravel 12 Vue 3 Sanctum Vite Docker
